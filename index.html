Introducing ESCAPE BEACH FEST! Camping

Experience the purest festival moments at the ESCAPE camping site. When you camp at Escape, youre not just attending the festival, you become a part of it. You get to live in community side-by-side with your fellow Headbangers. And for this moment in time, nowhere else matters.

We have a variety of camping options below, so take your pick! Just keep in mind that the more premium camping types tend to sell fastest due to limited capacity. The area will benefit from all the necessary conditions for security, hygiene, and accessibility.

To be part of the camping experience, you need a camping ticket . A camping ticket grants access to a single person, who can set up their tent within the designated area and use the provided resources. Two or more people can share a tent only if they have both camping tickets and festival passes.
